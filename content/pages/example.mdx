# Interview Checklist

Follow these steps to stay **prepared for success** in your next interview.

<ol className="list-decimal pl-6 space-y-2">
  <li>Research the company and role</li>
  <li>Prepare 2–3 STAR stories (Situation, Task, Action, Result)</li>
  <li>Practice answering common behavioral questions</li>
  <li>Draft thoughtful questions to ask the interviewer</li>
</ol>
